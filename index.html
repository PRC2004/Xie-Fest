SPANDAN


Spandan is the annual inter-collegiate cultural festival of Xavier Institute of Engineering, that is held every year in the month of January-February. "Spandan" is a Sanskrit word which means vibration. Welcome to our captivating Cultural Fest, where the world's cultures unite in a harmonious celebration of diversity!
@xie.spandan

TRANSMISSION


Transmission is the annual inter-collegiate technical festival of Xavier Institute of Engineering, that is held every year in the month of January-February. Welcome to our exhilarating Technical Fest, where innovation meets inspiration! Dive into a world of cutting-edge technology, science, and engineering.
@xie.transmission

SPARX


Sparx is the annual inter-collegiate sport festival of Xavier Institute of Engineering, that is held every year in the month of January-February. Welcome to our thrilling Sports Fest, where athleticism and sportsmanship take center stage! Get ready for an action-packed event that celebrates the spirit of competition and teamwork.
@xie.sparx
