SPANDAN

Spandan is the annual inter-collegiate cultural festival of Xavier Institute of Engineering, that is held every year in the month of January-February. "Spandan" is a Sanskrit word which means vibration. Welcome to our captivating Cultural Fest, where the world's cultures unite in a harmonious celebration of diversity! Join us on a journey through an array of artistic expressions, traditional performances, and culinary delights. This fest is a vibrant mosaic of global traditions, weaving together stories, melodies, and flavors from around the world. Immerse yourself in this cultural extravaganza that promotes cross-cultural understanding and appreciation. Let's come together to celebrate our shared heritage and the beauty of our differences!
@xie.spandan
TRANSMISSION

Transmission is the annual inter-collegiate technical festival of Xavier Institute of Engineering, that is held every year in the month of January-February. Welcome to our exhilarating Technical Fest, where innovation meets inspiration! Dive into a world of cutting-edge technology, science, and engineering. This fest is a platform for the brightest minds to showcase their creativity and expertise. Experience mind-boggling competitions, enlightening workshops, and awe-inspiring exhibitions. Whether you're a tech enthusiast, a budding engineer, or just curious about the future, our fest promises an electrifying journey through the world of innovation. Get ready to be amazed and inspired as we explore the limitless possibilities of technology together!
@xie.transmission
SPARX

Sparx is the annual inter-collegiate sport festival of Xavier Institute of Engineering, that is held every year in the month of January-February. Welcome to our thrilling Sports Fest, where athleticism and sportsmanship take center stage! Get ready for an action-packed event that celebrates the spirit of competition and teamwork. From heart-pounding races to intense matches, our fest is a showcase of dedication, skill, and passion. Whether you're a seasoned athlete or a sports enthusiast, there's something for everyone. Join us in the pursuit of victory, camaraderie, and the joy of sports. Let the games begin, and may the best team win!
@xie.sparx
